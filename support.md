## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to **Profile → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account**. This will permanently delete all your data including habits, streaks, health history, and purchases. This action cannot be undone.

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
On the login screen, tap **Forgot password?** and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

**Can I change my email address?**
Not currently — email changes aren't supported yet. Contact us at lirin.noreply@gmail.com if you need help with your account.

### Subscriptions & Purchases

**How do I manage my LIRIN Pro subscription?**
Your subscription is managed through Apple. Go to **iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → LIRIN** to cancel, upgrade, or view your billing details.

**I purchased a Streak Shield but it's not showing up.**
Force-quit the app and reopen it. If the shield still doesn't appear, contact us with your Apple purchase receipt and we'll sort it out.

**Can I restore my purchases on a new device?**
Yes — log into your LIRIN account on your new device. Your Pro status is tied to your LIRIN account. For Streak Shields (consumable purchases), these are stored on your account and will carry over.

**I deleted my account — can I restore my Pro subscription?**
No. Deleting your account permanently removes all data and purchase history. Your Apple subscription may still be active (cancel it through iPhone Settings to avoid future charges), but LIRIN cannot restore access or data after deletion.

**How do I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund for any App Store purchase.

### Apple Watch & Health Data

**My recovery score isn't updating.**
Make sure:
1. Your Apple Watch is charged and worn while sleeping
2. LIRIN has HealthKit access: **iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → LIRIN** — ensure all permissions are enabled
3. Open LIRIN and pull to refresh on the Recovery tab

**How do I connect my Apple Watch?**
LIRIN reads data automatically via Apple Health — there's no separate pairing needed. Just ensure HealthKit permissions are granted (see above).

**How do I disconnect from Apple Health?**
Go to **iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → LIRIN** and toggle off any data types you don't want LIRIN to access.

### Habits & Streaks

**My streak broke — what happened?**
Streaks break if you miss checking in on a habit for a full calendar day. You can protect a streak using a **Streak Shield** (available in Profile → Power-Ups).

**Can I restore a broken streak?**
Yes, if you have a Streak Shield available. Tap **Use Shield** on the affected habit card within 24 hours of missing a check-in.

**How do I delete a habit?**
Go to **Habits → Edit** and swipe left on the habit to delete it.

---

### Privacy & Data

**What data does LIRIN send to Anthropic (Claude AI)?**
Only anonymised health metrics (e.g. "HRV: 52ms, sleep: 7.2h"). We never send your name, email, or identifiable information. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy) for full details.

**How do I change my AI coaching consent?**
Go to **Profile → Privacy** in the app.

**How do I delete all my health data?**
Delete your account via **Profile → Delete Account**. This removes all data permanently.

### Strava

**How do I connect Strava?**
Go to **Coach → Strava tab → Connect Strava** and follow the OAuth flow.

**How do I disconnect Strava?**
Go to **Coach → Strava tab → Disconnect Strava**.
