# LIRIN Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 10, 2026**

LIRIN ("we", "our", or "us") is a wellness app developed by Madan Mohan Kumar Shanthakumar. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the LIRIN iOS app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)
With your explicit consent, LIRIN reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:
- Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep stages and duration
- Blood oxygen (SpO2)
- Breathing rate and respiratory rate
- Steps, calories burned, and activity data
- Workout history

This data is used solely to calculate your daily recovery score and generate personalised coaching insights. We never use HealthKit data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name and email address
- Password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your raw password)
- Timezone
- Optional: height, weight, fitness goals

### Usage Data
We collect basic usage information including:
- Habits, streaks, and check-in history
- Tasks and completion records
- Meal logs (including photos you choose to scan)
- Challenge participation and progress
- In-app purchases (Streak Shield, Pro subscription)

### Device Data
- Device push token (for habit reminder notifications)
- Apple Watch pairing status

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---|---|
| Recovery score calculation | HealthKit data |
| AI coaching responses | Anonymised health metrics, habits, tasks |
| Habit reminders | Push token, reminder times you set |
| Subscription management | Purchase receipts (via Apple StoreKit) |
| App personalisation | Goals, fitness level, body metrics |

### AI Coaching (Anthropic Claude)
LIRIN uses Claude, an AI made by Anthropic, to generate daily briefs and coaching responses. We send Anthropic **anonymised, aggregated** health metrics only — for example, "HRV: 52ms, sleep: 7.2h". We never send your name, email address, or any directly identifiable information to Anthropic. See [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

### Meal Photo Scanning
When you scan a meal photo, the image is sent to our server and to Anthropic's API for calorie and nutrient analysis. Photos are processed and are not stored permanently.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Your account data is stored on secure servers hosted by [Render.com](https://render.com) (International Servers).
- Health data synced from Apple HealthKit is stored in our database to generate your recovery score history.
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
- We do not store your raw Apple Health data beyond what is needed for your recovery history.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Anthropic** — anonymised health metrics for AI coaching (see Section 2)
- **Apple** — purchase verification via StoreKit (no personal data shared)
- **Render.com** — server hosting (your data is stored here)
- **Strava** — only if you choose to connect your Strava account

We will disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Correction
You can view and update your profile information at any time in the app under **Profile → Training**.

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time in the app under **Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account**. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Important:** Deleting your account will permanently remove all your habits, streaks, check-ins, health history, and profile data. Any active Pro subscriptions or purchased Streak Shields will be cancelled and cannot be restored.

### Manage Consent
You can change your AI coaching and HealthKit consent preferences at any time in the app under **Profile → Privacy**.

### Health Data
You can revoke LIRIN's access to Apple Health data at any time through **iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → LIRIN**.

### Notifications
You can disable habit reminders at any time through app settings or **iPhone Settings → Notifications → LIRIN**.
